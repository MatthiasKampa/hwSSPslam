SLAM lives on the FPGA system — the host is a shim. Every scan is encoded ON the FPGA (bit-exact vs golden, live); its compressed 2-bit codes live in the on-chip map bank, fetched + decoded here (purple layer). Lidar points scatter at the pose estimate (odometry live / offline est-cache on datasets; the on-chip tracker replaces both). Camera cells are the exported CNN's descriptor bits (C kernel) — query-by-example, no class labels.
